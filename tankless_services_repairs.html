What tankless water heater services do we offer?

We Offer Las Vegas Tankless Water Heater Installation

Kingdom Plumbing is a certified, experienced, and licensed installer of tankless water heaters in Las Vegas. We have been serving the communities and commercial establishments for over 3 generations, offering the highest standards of service with the best customer service you can find in the industry.

We do everything needed to make sure you get to experience the benefits of a tankless water heater system. We do each step of the process that includes;

· Removal of your old water heater
· Installation of the new tankless water heater
· Connecting to either a gas line or water supply
· Make sure all issues are properly checked, such as possible leaks, and other possible issues

 In other words, we make sure you get to have the best installation services in Las Vegas, with a tankless water heater you can use for 20 years or more.

We Offer Las Vegas Tankless Water Heater Repairs

If you already have a tankless water heater at home and want to have it looked at or repaired, we are the team to do the job right the first time. We can repair the following issues:
· Mineral Buildup
· No Ignition Message
 · Flame Failure
· Exhaust Blockage
· Air Supply Message

Fortunately, tankless water heats are more durable, more reliable, and more efficient They don’t require as much maintenance as regular water heaters. But in cases when there is an issue, or if you think there might be a problem, we have the experience, capacity, and servicemen who are experts at the job.

Call Kingdom Plumbing today for all of your tankless water heater needs and we will be more than happy to send one of our expert servicemen to your door.
